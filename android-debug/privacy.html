Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Dar Assalam app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect any personal information unless you choose to provide it (such as your name, age, or profile picture for user accounts). No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit your data to any external servers.

Permissions

The app may request permissions (such as storage or notifications) only to provide its core features. No data is accessed or used for any other purpose.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
